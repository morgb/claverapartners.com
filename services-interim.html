Services

Interim CFO & Finance Leadership

Some finance needs are defined by a moment - a leadership gap, a critical planning cycle, a transaction, or a system overhaul - where the right answer is experienced execution on a fixed timeline rather than a permanent hire. Clavera Partners is structured for exactly those situations, stepping in with a clear mandate and the experience to move quickly without the learning curve that typically slows an outside resource down.

Gap Coverage

When a CFO or senior finance leader departs unexpectedly, the demands on the finance function don't pause. Board reporting, lender packages, and team direction all continue regardless of what's happening at the leadership level. Clavera Partners provides stable, experienced interim leadership that keeps the function running at a high standard while the organization takes the time it needs to hire the right permanent candidate.

Project-Based Engagements

Not every need requires filling a seat - some require a specific outcome within a defined window. Clavera Partners works on project-based engagements across:

  • Annual budget builds and multi-year forecast models
  • Post-merger financial integration and restatement
  • Fundraise preparation and investor-ready financial packages
  • FP&A function buildout or rebuild
  • Cash flow stabilization and liquidity management
  • ERP or financial system implementation support

Each engagement is scoped clearly at the outset with defined deliverables, a realistic timeline, and a clean handoff at close.
