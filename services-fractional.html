Services

Fractional Finance Leadership

Not every company needs a full-time CFO or VP of Finance, but most growing businesses reach a point where they need the thinking, the discipline, and the hands-on execution that a senior finance leader brings. Clavera Partners provides ongoing, part-time finance leadership structured around a consistent weekly or monthly presence, with scope and depth adjusted to where the business actually is and what it genuinely needs.

CFO-Level Engagements

For companies that need an experienced financial thought partner at the leadership table - someone to own board reporting, drive capital structure decisions, manage lender and investor relationships, and bring discipline to long-term planning - without the cost or commitment of a full-time executive hire.

VP of Finance & FP&A Engagements

For companies that need someone actively in the work. Building models, running planning cycles, owning reporting, and managing the team. This level of engagement is designed for organizations that need real execution and analytical horsepower, not periodic check-ins from a distance.

Fractional engagements are retainer-based and built for continuity, with the goal of functioning as a genuine part of the leadership team rather than an outside resource. The right structure - whether CFO-level or VP-level - is determined by where the business is and what the finance function actually requires.

Not sure which level fits? Let's figure it out together.
