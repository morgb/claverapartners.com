Finance AI Readiness

AI is moving faster than most finance teams have been able to respond to, and the gap is rarely about willingness. CFOs are running the business, controllers are closing the books, and nobody has been given the time or the mandate to step back and evaluate where AI actually creates leverage in the finance function and how to get there in a practical, sequenced way. Clavera Partners fills that gap by bringing a CFO-level lens to the evaluation - grounded in how finance teams actually operate rather than what a technology vendor is trying to sell.

Finance AI Readiness engagements are scoped to give organizations a clear picture of where they are, where the real opportunities are, and what it will take to move. Engagements typically include:

  • Current state assessment of finance workflows, tools, and data infrastructure
  • Identification of highest-leverage AI opportunities by function - FP&A, reporting, close process, collections, and others
  • Honest evaluation of readiness across data quality, team capacity, and existing tool landscape
  • A prioritized roadmap with phased recommendations
  • Hands-on support through initial adoption and change management

The outcome is a finance team that is moving forward with clarity on what to do, in what order, and why - without the false starts that come from skipping the evaluation work.
