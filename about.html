About Clavera Partners

The financial partner founders and investors trust.

Clavera Partners is a boutique finance advisory firm working with founder-led and PE-backed companies at critical inflection points. We bring senior financial leadership to organizations ready to build the clarity and strategic rigor that matches where they're going - not just where they've been.

Our belief

Great financial leadership isn't about reporting on the past. It's about creating the visibility and confidence to make better decisions about the future. We work best when we're close to the business, close to the numbers, and close to the people who are accountable for both.

How we work

We come in at inflection points - a period of rapid growth, a business preparing to transact, a company ready for the rigor its next stage demands - and we stay until the work is done right. We don't drop a framework and move on. We build alongside you, roll up our sleeves when needed, and bring the financial discipline that turns good businesses into ones that scale with confidence.

The people behind the work

Morgan Muller

Founder & Principal

Morgan brings over 16 years of senior finance experience across private equity and operating CFO roles. She has built finance functions from the ground up, guided companies through periods of rapid growth and transition, and partnered with founders, CEOs, and boards to create lasting financial clarity.

Prior to founding Clavera Partners, Morgan served as CFO of IT ServicePoint and VP of Finance at FEV Tutor. Before moving into operating roles, she spent time at Dorilton Capital working directly with portfolio companies - embedded in their operations, driving value creation, and building the financial infrastructure that supported growth and investment outcomes.

Her earlier career spanned investment banking at Bank of America Merrill Lynch in London and deal advisory at KPMG in New York - experience that shapes how she thinks about capital structure, transactions, and value creation today.
